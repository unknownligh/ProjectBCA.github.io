Transport

For the transportation of the day-scholar students and staff the college has 55 buses plying through every nook and corner of Hosur town and its suburbs like Krishnagiri, Kaveripatanam, Thirupathur, Jolarpet, Bagulur, Thenkanikotai etc. The bus routes are operated after taking into account the need of the students. All students make use of the college transport or stay in the hostel.
All the buses reach the campus before 8:30 a.m. so that students and faculty can have their breakfast and get ready to attend the classes at 8:30 a.m. The buses leave the campus at 4:00 pm, which helps students to reach home early and safe. The time mentioned below is the indicative time. The Students are asked to come to the boarding point, 10 minutes before the time indicated.

NSS(National Service Scheme)

NSS, or the National Service Scheme, is a voluntary community service program in colleges across India. It aims to instill the spirit of social responsibility and civic engagement in students. participating in NSS typically have dedicated units or cells that organize various community service activities and initiatives.
NSS activities often include voluntary work in areas such as education, healthcare, environmental conservation, and community development. Students enrolled in NSS engage in projects that benefit local communities, addressing social issues and contributing to the overall well-being of society.
Participating in NSS not only allows students to make a positive impact on the community but also provides them with valuable opportunities for personal development, leadership, and teamwork. It fosters a sense of social awareness and encourages a commitment to social service beyond the academic environment. Overall, NSS plays a vital role in shaping responsible and compassionate citizens during their college years.

Canteen

The canteen is an important facility for students because it provides high-quality food and refreshments. Canteens also serve as informal gathering places for students to unwind with a relaxing conversation. It is the most popular location on campus, not only for physical refreshment but also for student bonding and discussions.
The College canteen is one of the busiest spots in the campus. The canteen is located on the ground floor of Building III of the College. The canteen has a large kitchen, and the kitchen staff goes out of their way to provide students and staff with nutritious and hygienic food on campus. Our canteen strives to offer a wide range of high-quality food and beverages at reasonable prices. Students receive subsidized food from 7.30 a.m. to 6.30 p.m.
The Canteen served a variety of Jain food to the students and staff of the College. It also provides refreshment like fresh Juices, snack items, sandwiches, beverages etc. Food items are prepared with RO treated water. RO water purifiers are also installed in the Canteen.

Student Stationary Store

A college stationary store is an essential resource for students, offering a variety of supplies to support their academic pursuits. These stores typically stock a range of items such as notebooks, pens, pencils, folders, binders, textbooks, and other stationery essentials.
Additionally, they may provide technology accessories, art supplies, and college-branded merchandise. College stationary stores aim to meet the diverse needs of students, helping them stay organized, take notes, and complete assignments effectively.
Some stores may also offer services like photocopying, printing, and access to course materials. Overall, these stores play a crucial role in providing students with the tools and resources necessary for a successful and productive academic experience.

Medical

Medical support in college involves a range of services and facilities aimed at promoting and maintaining the health of students. College campuses typically have health centers or clinics staffed with healthcare professionals, including doctors, nurses, and other medical personnel. These facilities offer primary care services, administer vaccinations, provide first aid, and address common health issues.In the event of illnesses or injuries, students can seek medical attention at the campus health center.
Additionally, college medical support may include mental health services, counseling, and resources for students dealing with emotional or psychological challenges. This holistic approach to medical support acknowledges the interconnectedness of physical and mental well-being. Overall, the goal is to create a supportive environment that prioritizes the health and well-being of students throughout their academic journey.
