Sports

About Sports

MGR College recognises the importance of the saying, “ All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy.” This spirit of ours is reflected through the amenities that we have in the campus to build sports culture among the students. The college has an extensive playground of 5 acres which serves multipurpose. The institution’s state-of the-art infrastructure supports both indoor and outdoor sports and games. With different games on hand, we have well-maintained play area for Football, Hockey, Cricket, Kabbadi and Kho-Kho. In addition to this, there are two concrete Basketball courts, three Volleyball courts and two tennis courts, which are available all the time for student’s access.The exquisite auditorium facilitates indoor games as it houses carom and chess area, table-tennis court and boxing zone. The dedicated 1200 mts track for athletics is a cherry on the cake. Along with sports utilities, we provide Gym facility to enhance student’s fitness.

The result of our work towards developing sports have exemplified in our students bringing rich laurels by participating in the various inter and intra collegiate sports meet, Zonal, District and National events. It is noteworthy to mention that our college holds the maximum number of representations in the Periyar University team . We also provide free accommodation and food to the students who are part of the University team thereby encouraging and ensuring their participation in the sports activities.
