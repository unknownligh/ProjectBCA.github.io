Training And Placement

Placement Cell

The MGR College Placement Cell aims at making available quality placement opportunities for the students by maintaining linkages with Human Resource Departments in Industry and Research Institutions. The alumni of the institute also play a dynamic role in this venture. The placement cell is headed by two Training and Placement Officers (TPOs) and has a faculty representative from each department under the MGR College umbrella. These representatives are the conveners of student and faculty level training.

Vision

Excellence in Career Counseling and Placement.

Mission

To achieve excellence in placement by having perpetual interaction with industries and provide an avenue for an employment.

Objectives

To make the students employable and competent to meet the global standards.

About Us

The placement cell is responsible for maintaining contact with industries specific to their specialization and for arranging placement visits to the campus. Apart from faculty representation the students from each class represent as placement coordinators. Numerous training programmes were conducted for a period of 6 months by 50+ trainers with an objective of exposing the students to know the importance of career counseling and placement. The prime focus of the training was to update the students with the skills that are required to cater the needs in career development. In the training programme special emphasis was given in the areas related to aptitude, soft skills, stress management, group discussion and over all personality development. Activity based training and mock interviews were also conducted to help the students to fetch knowledge with respect to the demands and expectation of the industries related to placement.
