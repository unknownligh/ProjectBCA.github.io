Hostel

About Hostel

The Institution has three hostels (Two for Boys & One for Girls). Each hostel can accommodate 500 residents. There are separate Dining Hall with a capacity of 400 each.It has all the infrastructural facilities like well furnished rooms, modern kitchen with clean dining halls, water heatersm indoot and outdoor games, library and computer centre. An open air theatre is provided within the Ladies Hostel.The hostel also has cultural clubs to promote extra curricular activities.
